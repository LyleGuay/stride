3 Context menu gains "Add to Favorites"

"Add to Favorites" is added between Duplicate and the separator. For already-favorited items, it becomes Remove from Favorites.

This gives two paths to favorite: the hover star (fast, in-context) and the context menu (always accessible, even without hovering).

The Add Item modal would also get a small "★ Save as favorite" checkbox near the submit button for capturing new favorites during logging.
