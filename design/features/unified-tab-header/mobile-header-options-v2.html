What other apps do

Common patterns observed across iOS and Android productivity and social apps.

Scrollable chips below header YouTube, Google Maps, Google News

Module name is prominent in its own header row (h-14). A full-width scrollable chip/pill row sits directly below — chips are the tab switcher. No underline tabs. Works well when there are many tabs. Feels like content filtering rather than navigation.

New ideas for Stride

Tailored to Stride's sidebar-first layout and the specific request.

★ Name left · Tab dropdown right Your idea — similar to iOS Mail folder picker

Module name anchors the left. Active tab name + chevron on the right acts as a dropdown trigger. Tap it to switch tabs. Maximum horizontal space, no second row needed. Works identically for 2 or 5 tabs.
