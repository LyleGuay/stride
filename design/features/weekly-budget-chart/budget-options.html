Weekly Budget Chart — Display Options

Scenario: Budget was 2,300 Mon–Wed, then changed to 2,000 Thu–Sun

Current (single line)

One horizontal line — doesn't reflect mid-week budget change

Problem: Thu/Fri appear "under budget" vs 2,300 line, but their actual budget was 2,000

Option A — Stepped line

Line steps down when budget changes. Each segment = that day's budget

Line visibly steps down on Thursday when budget changed. Fri correctly shows as over.

Option B — Per-bar tick marks

Small notch on each bar at that day's budget level. No connecting line.

Disconnected — harder to see "the budget changed here." Clean but less readable at a glance.

Option C — Budget "ghost" bar

Faint bar shows budget height; actual net bar overlaid on top

Shows budget as context behind each bar. More visual noise but budget is always obvious.
